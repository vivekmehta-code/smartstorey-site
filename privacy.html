Privacy Policy — Smart Storey

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Smart Storey ("we", "us") manufactures and supplies hotel furniture in India. This policy explains how we handle information when you contact us, including via WhatsApp after clicking one of our advertisements.

Information we collect

When you message us on WhatsApp or contact us by phone, we may collect: your name, phone number, hotel or business name, city, and details of your furniture requirement (such as number of rooms, budget range, and timeline). We also keep a record of our conversation with you so we can serve you better.

How we use it

We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry, prepare quotations, arrange calls or samples, and follow up on your requirement. Parts of our WhatsApp responses are generated with the assistance of automated (AI) tools, supervised by our team. We may use aggregate, anonymised data to improve our advertising and service.

What we don't do

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except the service providers that make our operations possible (messaging, data storage, and AI processing providers), who process it only on our instructions.
